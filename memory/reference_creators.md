## What these three references signal together

Max is NOT drawn to fast-cut, hook-bait content. All three references are:
- Calm and intentional
- Depth-first (lesson/insight over entertainment)
- Low-ego (documenting reality, not performing success)
- Visually intentional but not production-obsessed

**Strategic tension to flag:** The Elias Maman framework in the knowledge base is built for comment-bait and fast hooks. That's a LinkedIn play. For Instagram, Max's references point toward a narrated, cinematic, slower style — closer to hfxdrifter than Elias Maman.

**Production reality for Reels (hfxdrifter model):**
1. Write the voiceover script first (60–90 words max)
2. Shoot 6–8 clips of daily life, movement, or environment (iPhone, no tripod needed)
3. Record voiceover separately (even in a quiet room on phone)
4. Cut B-roll to voiceover rhythm in CapCut or Premiere
5. Apply warm film LUT (free options available)
6. Add one music track underneath

Max can do all of this. His editing background is an asset here, not a liability — as long as he doesn't let step 4 become a 6-hour session.
